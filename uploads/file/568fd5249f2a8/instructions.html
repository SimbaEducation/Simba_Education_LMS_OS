Instructions

Material Needed

  1. Bingo cards

  2. Small object: for example: buttons, small sweets, coins, small stones. Each student must have enough small objects to fill the whole card.

Step by Step

  1. Hand out a bingo card to each student in the group.

  2. Explain the instructions of how the game works to the group.

  3. Select a game number. This is to ensure a different set and order of numbers is called every time the game is played.

  4. Call out the letters or numbers on the screen in the given order. Only call one number at a time.

  5. If the student has the character called, they are to place the small object on top of the character.

  6. The winner is the one who covers their entire card.

  7. Once a player has completed their card, they must shout out ‘Bingo.’

  8. When a player shouts ‘BINGO’ check the card to ensure they identified the correct characters/

  9. Allow multiple winners.
