Preparation

30 minutes to prepare this activity

Context

Most of the preparation is required the first time the activity is carried out. Preparation materials must be kept, as this is a recurrent activity. Create your classâ€™s bingo cards.

  • Take an A4 sheet of paper, place it landscape on the table and cut it in half.
  • On the A5 size of paper, draw the Bingo template with a black marker. 4 rows and 5 columns
  • Multiple games are provided so that a different order is used when ever the game is played.

Tips

  • In this game, the person who calls is the Bingo Master. To reward a student, you can appoint them as the bingo master. This can be a rotational role that encourages leadership and confidence.
  • Keep the game fun! It is not a completion
  • Assist students who are struggling to recognize the characters by giving them clues and showing them.

Template

Write any letter between A and M in each block. The board must be a combination of letters and numbers. Majority must be letters.

Ensure you mix up the order of the letters. You may repeat some letters within one template.
